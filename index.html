Hybrid Assistant App
Datamining Hybrid Assistant

In winter 2019, my 2006 Prius broke down. After this ordeal I sought a way to monitor my trips. In April 2019 I started datalogging using a ELM327 v1.3a OBDII bluetooth module and a PH-1 android phone. This site is my analysis of the data I have gather so far.

This is a living page and will develop over time.
